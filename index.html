About INDIAN LASER TECHNIK (I L T)

Established in 2018, Indian Laser Technik (ILT) specializes in the manufacturing of the sheet and tubes Fiber Laser Cutting Machines. The company also provides CNC Press Brake Machines and a wide range of laser consumables. Under the leadership of Mr. Mahesh Bhimrao Katkar, ILT has built a strong reputation for delivering high-precision machines, reliable solutions, and excellent service while maintaining strong ethical business practices.

Our Machines

Fiber Laser Cutting Machine

Full Body Pallet Changer

Automatic pallet loading and unloading system for higher productivity.

CNC Hydraulic Press Brake

Precision sheet metal bending machine using CNC hydraulic technology.

Tube Fiber Laser Cutting Machine

High precision cutting solution for metal tubes and pipes.

Laser Consumables

Laser Cutting Heads

High precision laser heads including BOCI, Raytool and Precitec for advanced fiber laser cutting machines.

Laser Sources

Reliable laser sources from MAX, Raycus and IPG ensuring stable performance and high cutting quality.

Laser Control Systems

Advanced control systems such as CypCut and Raytool for precise and efficient machine operation.

Laser Lenses

High-quality laser lenses from Boshe, Raytools, Precitec, WSX and Ospri for precise beam focusing and consistent cutting performance.
